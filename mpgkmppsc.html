Chapter 11 · Gathan Itihaas — Formation History (1947 → 2023)
1947: Central Provinces & Berar + Baghelkhand + Chhattisgarh → "Madhya Pradesh" (Category A). Capital: Nagpur.
1947: Western princely states → "Madhya Bharat" (Category B). Capitals: Indore & Gwalior.
1947: Northern states → "Vindhya Pradesh" (Category C). Capital: Rewa. Bhopal = separate princely state.
State Reorganisation Commission: Fazal Ali (Chairman), Hridaynath Kunzru, Dr. K.M. Panikkar. Recommended new MP from 1 Nov 1956.
1 Nov 1956 — OUT: Buldhana, Akola, Amravati, Yavatmal, Wardha, Nagpur, Bhandara, Chanda → joined Bombay State.
1 Nov 1956 — IN: Bhanpura of Mandsaur (except Sunel Tapla) | Kota's Sironje Tehsil (Rajasthan) → Vidisha, MP.
1 Nov 1956: Bhopal + Vindhya Pradesh + Madhya Bharat merged → New MP with 43 districts. Capital: Bhopal.
26 Jan 1972: Bhopal & Rajnandgaon created → 45 districts.
1998: Districts increased to 61 via two batches of new districts.
★ 1 November 2000 — CRITICAL: Chhattisgarh became 26th state. MP Vidhan Sabha: 320 → 230 seats.
24 May 2008: Singrauli = 50th district (carved from Sidhi).
2013: Agar Malwa = 51st district (from Shajapur).
24 May 2018: Niwari = 52nd district (from Tikamgarh).
★ 2023 — LATEST: Mauganj (Rewa) = 53rd | Pandhurna (Chhindwara) = 54th | Maihar (Satna) = 55th → TOTAL: 55 districts.
Approved Pending (18 Mar 2020): Chachaura & Nagda — not yet officially formed as of March 2026.
