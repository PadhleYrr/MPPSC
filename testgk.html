Chapter 11 · Gathan Itihaas — Formation History (1947→2023)
1947: Central Provinces & Berar + Baghelkhand + Chhattisgarh → MP (Category A). Capital: Nagpur.
1947: Western states → Madhya Bharat (Category B). Capitals: Indore & Gwalior.
1947: Northern states → Vindhya Pradesh (Category C). Capital: Rewa. Bhopal = separate state.
SRC: Fazal Ali (Chairman), Hridaynath Kunzru, K.M. Panikkar → recommended new MP from 1 Nov 1956.
1 Nov 1956 — OUT: Buldhana, Akola, Amravati, Yavatmal, Wardha, Nagpur, Bhandara, Chanda → Bombay State.
1 Nov 1956: Bhopal + Vindhya Pradesh + Madhya Bharat merged → New MP, 43 districts. Capital: Bhopal.
26 Jan 1972: 45 districts. | 1998: 61 districts. | 2001–08: 64 districts.
★ 1 Nov 2000 CRITICAL: Chhattisgarh = 26th state. MP VS: 320 → 230.
2008: Singrauli = 50th | 2013: Agar Malwa = 51st | 2018: Niwari = 52nd
★ 2023: Mauganj (53rd) + Pandhurna (54th) + Maihar (55th) → TOTAL: 55 districts
